    Description:
      Abstract:
        This map layer portrays the Congressional districts of the United States, Puerto Rico, and 
        the U.S. Virgin Islands, for the 114th Congress.  
      Purpose:
        These data are intended for geographic display and analysis at the national level, and for large 
        regional areas.  The data should be displayed and analyzed at scales appropriate 
        for 1:1,000,000-scale data.  No responsibility is assumed by the U.S. Geological Survey in the use 
        of these data.
      Supplemental_Information:
        Many States have more detailed Congressional district information available online; please contact 
        the applicable State government for further information.

      Process_Step:
        Process_Description: 
          Starting with the data from CD113, the lists of Congressional representatives and senators were 
          updated using the Freshman114, House, and Senate data sources.  There are no Congressional 
          District boundary changes between the 113th and 114th Congress.

          In CD113, the Hawaii second district was mapped as multiple features.  Those features were merged 
          into a single multi-part feature.

          The map layer was saved in shapefile and in file geodatabase formats.
